About Us

Who Are We?

At Ambrose Treacy College, we are a vibrant and inclusive community dedicated to nurturing young minds and empowering them to reach their full potential. As a premier educational institution, we are committed to providing a holistic learning experience that fosters academic excellence, character development, and a strong sense of social responsibility.

Our dedicated team of educators and staff work tirelessly to create a safe and supportive environment where students can thrive intellectually, emotionally, and spiritually. With a rich history and a forward-thinking approach to education, we embrace innovation and foster a love for learning that extends beyond the classroom.

What We Do?

We are dedicated to fostering an inclusive and vibrant learning community that nurtures the holistic development of young minds. Our mission is to inspire and empower each student to reach their full potential, academically, emotionally, and socially.

Through a dynamic and innovative curriculum, we strive to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and a love for lifelong learning. Our experienced and passionate educators are committed to providing personalized support and guidance to every student, ensuring they thrive in their educational journey.

Why We Do It?

Our driving force is to foster holistic growth and inspire excellence in every aspect of education. We firmly believe in creating a vibrant and inclusive learning environment that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit of each student.

Our unwavering commitment to academic excellence ensures that students receive a well-rounded education, empowering them to embrace challenges with confidence and adaptability. By promoting critical thinking, creativity, and innovation, we prepare our students to be lifelong learners and future leaders.

How We Do It?

Our dedicated team of educators utilizes cutting-edge teaching methodologies to encourage critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. With a focus on individualized attention, we ensure that every student receives the support they need to excel.

Our state-of-the-art facilities provide a dynamic and engaging setting for learning, enabling students to explore their interests across a range of subjects, including arts, sciences, and sports. We also encourage a strong sense of community, fostering values of respect, compassion, and integrity among our students.

Edmund Rice Education Australia

Established in 2007 by the Christian Brothers, EREA is a network of schools, entities and offices that offer a Catholic education in the tradition of its founder, Blessed Edmund Rice. In 2014 there are almost 50 schools administered by EREA, enrolling over 35,000 students throughout Australia.
